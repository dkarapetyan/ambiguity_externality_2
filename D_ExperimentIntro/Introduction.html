Overview of Study
Here is a brief overview of the study.

What will I have to do?
This study will be divided into two parts. Each part consists of two main tasks along with brief survey questions.

You will now participate in Part I. After you have made your decisions in Part I, the computer program will prompt you to start Part 2 of the study.

Parts 1 and 2 together should take an average of 30 minutes to complete in total.

How much payment will I receive for my participation?
You will receive {{ session.config.participation_fee }} for completing both Part 1 and Part 2 of the study.

You may receive additional bonus payments depending on the decisions you make, and the decisions other individuals make who are paired with you in either Part 1 or Part 2. At the end of the study, the computer program will randomly pick either Part I or Part 2 to determine your bonus payment. Since nobody knows which part will be selected for payment, you should pay close attention to both parts as your decisions may determine your earnings.

How will payment be made?
Once all participants complete this study, we will determine bonus payments based on the decisions you and your paired counterpart made in the tasks in either Part I or Part 2. The bonus payment will be made via the Prolific platform within a maximum of 21 days from the conclusion of the study.

You will receive the completion payment of {{ session.config.participation_fee }} and your bonus payment only if you complete both Part 1 and Part 2 of the study.

Please note!
There will be several Attention Check questions throughout this study meant to test whether you are paying attention. If you fail to correctly complete one or more of these Attention Check questions, you may not be paid.
